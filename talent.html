How to Enter The competion

1• Upload your entry video: Record a 90-second video showcasing your talent and upload it to your personal Facebook page

2• Make sure to tag @Price Paint Factory Direct in your post. Include necessary details: In your video caption, include your full names, age, and school. Don't forget to tag @Price Paint Factory Direct and use the hashtag #PricePaintTalent

3• Enter on behalf of under-age entrants: If you're under 18 or don't have a Facebook page, ask your legal guardian to create an account and upload your video on their behalf, including the required details.

4• Wait for us to upload your video: We'll take 24-48 hours to upload your video to our YouTube channel.

5• Find and subscribe to our YouTube channel: Look for your video on our YouTube channel and subscribe to our channel.

6• Share your video with friends and family: Promote your video by sharing it with your loved ones.

7• Video with the most likes on Youtube will win
