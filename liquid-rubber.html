Liquid Roof Rubber is applied to roofs with minor leaks and possible rust. The thickness of liquid Roof Rubber can range from 5mm ( 5-7m2 per L) Unlike traditional roof paint, Liquid Roof Rubber offers a smooth seamless seal, flexibility and great adhesion.
