Description

Our price paint rubber road marking is engineered to flex even at extremely low temperatures from below zero to the extreme upper celsius range. Its thixotropic properties ensures a high micron build after application, providing excellent resistance against wear and tear. For clients who prefer solvent based road marking paint, price paint offers this option. Developed to be extremely fast drying (5-10 minutes) it is hard wearing, providing on both asphalt and concrete surfaces. Price paint rubber road marking paint is the perfect paint for road, concrete and factory markings.
